Our Heritage

Cooking from the Heart Since 1987

What started as Nonna Rosa's kitchen table has grown into a beloved dining destination, but our recipes and values remain unchanged. Every dish is crafted with the same love, patience, and locally-sourced ingredients as the first day we opened.

We believe that great food brings families together. That's why every corner of La Famiglia is designed to feel like home — warm, welcoming, and filled with the aroma of something wonderful.
